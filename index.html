Filmmakers Focused

Thru the Lens Film Festival will feature around 20 short films that are innovative in storytelling and cinematography. TTLFF believes that there are filmmakers that are passionate in their field, and TTLFF wants to showcase them. Plus, it is a great event to network with other filmmakers and crew.

SUBMIT YOUR FILM

TTLFF knows that there is plenty of talent out there. There are six categories to consider for submission: Ya'll Texans, Narrative, Women Voices, Horror, Student Short and Comedy. After each block, we will have a Q&A to talk for filmmakers. Films need to be 30 minutes or less. Submit your film at FilmFreeway Site and be part of the TTLFF.

Not accepting submission at this time.

Film Fest Movie-goers

TTLFF will show up to five short films for each of six category block. Selected films will be chosen due to inventiveness in storytelling and/or cinematography. TTLFF will have actors and directors round table session to talk about their creativity. A chance for audience to see a mind of a filmmaker or actor. Tickets sale coming soon.
