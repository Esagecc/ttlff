Filmmakers Focused

Prepare for an electrifying experience at the Thru the Lens Film Festival! With around 20 boundary-pushing short films, expect to be captivated by unique storytelling and cinematography. Join passionate filmmakers at our festival, a prime showcase for their talent, and seize the chance to network within the filmmaking community. Don't miss this unforgettable celebration of innovation and artistry!

SUBMIT YOUR FILM

TTLFF knows that there is plenty of talent out there. There are six categories to consider for submission: Y'all Texans, Narrative, Women Voices, Horror, Student Short and LGBTQ+. After each block, we will have a Q&A to talk for filmmakers. Films need to be 30 minutes or less. Submit your film at FilmFreeway Site and be part of the TTLFF.

Early Bird submissions starts on July 1, 2024!!!

Film Fest Movie-goers

TTLFF will show up to five short films for each of six category block. Selected films will be chosen due to inventiveness in storytelling and/or cinematography. TTLFF will have actors and directors round table session to talk about their creativity. A chance for audience to see a mind of a filmmaker or actor.
