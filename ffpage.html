Congratulation to Selected Films

We extend our appreciation to all filmmakers who have submitted their films. Completing a film and submitting it to film festivals represents a significant achievement. Our curated program showcases films that excel in storytelling and cinematography. We are pleased to acknowledge the filmmakers whose works have been selected for our program, and many of them will be present to discuss their artistic endeavors.
