Spring 2024

Great Short Films Everywhere

Thru the Lens Film Festival will feature various shorts films that are innovative in storytelling and cinematography. TTLFF believes that there are upcoming directors that are bound to break into the industry, and we want dibs. Plus, it is a great event to network with other filmmakers and crew. The passion for films will make every festival memorable.

For Flix-Loving Audience

TTLFF will be showing five short films for each of five category blocks: Ya'll Texans, Narrative, Women Voices, Horror and Comedy. Selected films will be chosen due to inventiveness in storytelling and/or cinematics. A Q&A is completed at the end of the category block. Here is your chance to see a mind of a filmmaker. Buy your tickets at eventbee.

For Filmmakers

Austin is an upcoming movie industry city due to various film studios, Hollywood directors and actors around the corner. TTLFF wants to show that there is plenty of talent out there. There are five categories to consider for submission. Submit your film at FilmFreeway Sites and be part of the TTLFF.
