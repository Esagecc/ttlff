Thru the Lens Film Festival will feature 25 shorts films that are innovative in storytelling and cinematography. TTLFF believes that there are upcoming directors that are bound to break into the industry, and TTLFF wants to showcase them. Plus, it is a great event to network with other filmmakers and crew. The passion for filmmaking will make every festival memorable.

SUBMIT YOUR FILM

TTLFF knows that there is plenty of talent out there. There are five categories to consider for submission: Ya'll Texans, Narrative, Women Voices, Horror and Comedy. After each block, we will have a Q&A to talk for filmmakers. Films need to be 30 minutes or less. Submit your film at FilmFreeway Site and be part of the TTLFF.

Earlybird deadline is September 20, 2023!

Fest Movie-goers

TTLFF will show five short films for each of five category block. Selected films will be chosen due to inventiveness in storytelling and/or cinematography. TTLFF will have actors and directors round table session to talk about their creativity. A chance for audience to see a mind of a filmmaker or actor. Tickets sale coming soon.
